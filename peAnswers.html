MASTERY TEST 1.1

Which type of fitness is not based on performance?

FLEXIBILITY


What is the ability of the body to function successfully and efficiently during physical activity?

PHYSICAL FITNESS


Which component of physical fitness is skill-related?

AGILITY


What is the purpose of the information in the red box on this nutrition label?

It defines the recommended amount to eat of a particular food.


What is the purpose of setting a deadline for a goal?

To help better define the goal.


How does cardiorespiratory endurance affect physical fitness?

It increases the efficiency with which the body delivers oxygen and nutrients to its cells.


Jordan exercises one to two times a week. She usually works out alone. Between meals, she sometimes snacks on potato chips from the bag. Which suggestion would least improve Jordan's overall fitness?

Eat more meals at restaurants (I believe, I unfortunately haven't been able to check).


Which method will provide the most emotional support in a new exercise program?

Trying the new exercise program with a friend.
