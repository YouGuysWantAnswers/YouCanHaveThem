Swapnil is experiencing dehydration. What is MOST likely happening?

B. He is sweating out more fluid than he is consuming.


Andre’s trainer tells him to do bodyweight exercises. Which activity would BEST help Andre accomplish this goal?

C. doing push-ups and squats


Olivia is experiencing hypoglycemia. What is MOST likely the cause of this condition?

D. She didn’t eat enough calories before her workout.

Karine’s doctor is putting her on a treadmill and having her jog for 10 minutes. The doctor wants to see how well her heart and lungs function. What is the doctor MOST likely trying to measure?

B. her cardiovascular endurance


For 10 seconds Ariana places her finger on her radial artery and counts her heartbeats. In 10 seconds, she counts 15 beats. What is Ariana’s current heart rate based on this measurement?

B. 90

C. muscular strength


Marta’s main fitness goals are to increase flexibility and decrease stress. Which activity would BEST help her meet her fitness goals?

D. Pilates
